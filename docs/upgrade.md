## Upgrade guide
  * `composer update unisharp/laravel-filemanager`
  * `php artisan vendor:publish --tag=lfm_view --force`
  * `php artisan vendor:publish --tag=lfm_config --force` (IMPORTANT: please backup your own `config/lfm.php` first)
